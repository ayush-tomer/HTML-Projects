The Best Movies According to Ayush Tomer

My top 10 movies of all-time

Harry Potter

Release Year: 2001-2011

Director: Chris Columbus, Alfonso Cuaron, Mike Newell, and David Yates

IMDB Rating: 7.5-8.1

This is a series of 8 magical fantasy movies.

Interstellar

Release Year: 2014

Director: Christopher Nolan

IMDB Rating: 8.7

It is an epic sci-fi movie known for its breathtaking visuals.

Home alone

Release Year: 1990-2012

Director: Chris Columbus, Raja Gosnell, Rod Daniel, and Peter Hewitt

IMDB Rating: 2.6-7.7

This ia series of movies which contains very creative content.

Baby's Day Out

Release Year: 1994

Director: Patrick Read Jhonson

IMDB Rating: 6.2

It is a funny movie which proves that babies are afraid of nothing.

Mrs. Doubtfire

Release Year: 1993

Director: Chris Columbus

IMDB Rating: 7.1

Although this is a comedy movie but it has important message to deliver.

Drive

Release Year: 2011

Director: Nicolas Winding Refn

IMDB Rating: 7.8

Super beautiful movie. Really artistic.

Ex Machina

Release Year: 2014

Director: Alex Garland

IMDB Rating: 7.7

Really cool sci-fi movie.

Spirited Away

Release Year: 2001

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

IMDB Rating: 8.6

This is my favourite anime movie. I love the beautiful images. This will take you totally to another world.

The Shawshank Redemption

Release Year: 1994

Director: Frank Darabont

IMDB Rating: 9.3

It is a movie having story of hope, friendship, and resilience. It is a powerful meditation on the human spirit's ability to endure, even in the darkest places.

The Godfather

Release Year: 1972

Director: Francis Ford Coppola

IMDB Rating: 9.2

It is an epic crime film. It is known for its deep character studies, moral complexity, and exploration through the lens of crime and power.
